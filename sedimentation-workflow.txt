Sedimentation Workflow

STEP 1 - Mesh Refinements
- input: simulationID, dim, ncellx, ncelly, ncellz, xmin, ymin, zmin, xmax, ymax, zmax, ref_interval
- output: simulationID, r_fraction, c_fraction, max_h_level, hlevels, first_step_refinement

STEP 2 - Create Equation Systems - Dependency: STEP 1

- output: simulationID, Reynolds, Gr, Sc, Us, Diffusivity, xlock, alfa, theta, ex, ey, ez, c_factor

STEP 3 - Get Maximum Iterations - Dependency: STEP 2

- output: simulationID, dt,tmax,n_time_steps,n_nonlinear_steps,nonlinear_tolerance,max_linear_iters,max_r_steps,write_interval

STEP 4 - Solver Simulation to the Fluid - Dependency: STEP 3

- output: simulationID, t_step,transport_system.time,r,l,n_linear_iterations,final_linear_residual,norm_delta,norm_delta/u_norm,converged

STEP 5 - Solver Simulation to the Sediments - Dependency: STEP 4

- output: simulationID, t_step,transport_system.time,r,l,n_linear_iterations,final_linear_residual,norm_delta,norm_delta/u_norm,converged
